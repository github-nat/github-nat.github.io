EXPLORE THE BRAND MARK

The Transurban brand mark appears on all of our corporate communications. It comprises a logotype (text) and symbol arrangement and should always be used as a single graphic unit.

COLOUR

The brand mark should always contrast with the background. There are five versions of the brand mark to ensure optimal legibility across all applications.

Full colour

The full colour brand mark is the preferred version to be used.

Mono black

The mono black version may only be reproduced in black. No other colours are allowed.

Reverse two colour

This reverse two colour version may only be reproduced on black backgrounds or grey of no less than 80 per cent density.

Reverse mono

The reversed mono brand mark may only be used on a black background or a background (photo or graphic) that has a flat colour density of no less than 35 per cent of its full colour strength.

Black Halftone

The black halftone brand mark is intended for use where it is not practical to reproduce the brand mark in its full colour version. e.g. newsprint

CLEAR SPACE

Clear space defines the minimum area that must be left clear around the brand mark to ensure it is never overshadowed by other text or visual elements. This promotes clarity and improves overall impact. Clear space is scaled in proportion to the logo size.

MINIMUM SIZE

The brand mark should not be reproduced smaller then 30mm in width to ensure optimal reproduction quality across all applications.

Exception

The only exception to this rule is business cards where the absolute minimum size is 10mm (width).
